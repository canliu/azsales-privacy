Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

✓ This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Overview

Amazon BSR Sales Estimator is a Chrome browser extension that predicts daily sales estimates for products on Amazon search results pages. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser. No user data is ever sent to external servers.

What the Extension Does

The extension reads publicly visible product information — including BSR ranking, category, price, and reviews — from Amazon search results pages already displayed in your browser. This data is used solely to calculate sales estimates and is never sent to any external server.

Third Party Services

The extension does not connect to any external servers or third-party services. No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used.
