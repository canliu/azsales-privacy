Privacy Policy

Amazon BSR Sales Estimator
Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Amazon BSR Sales Estimator is a Chrome browser extension. This policy explains how the extension handles data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What the Extension Does

The extension reads publicly visible product information (BSR ranking, category, price, reviews) from Amazon search results pages already displayed in your browser. This data is used solely to calculate sales estimates and is never sent to any external server.

Third Party Services

The extension does not connect to any external servers or third-party services. No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used.
